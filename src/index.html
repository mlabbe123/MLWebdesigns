The Band

The independent and bone-shattering sextet KillaWail features members from both sides of the globe; Montreal and Melbourne, Australia. Combining talent from both countries, the group combines a heavy-riffing rhythm core with a smoking 3-piece horn section, playing their brand of Garage/Soul and wrecking dance floors across Canada.

Their music is defined by the combination of heavily syncopated horn grooves with intricate, Latin-infused drum patterns that give way to thumping rock beats. With a bass guitar and synth locking down the bottom end complemented by fast and raw blues-guitar riffs, the band confidently delivers a sound that has been likened to a mixture between The Arctic Monkeys jamming with the Dap-Kings and a touch of the Black Keys.
